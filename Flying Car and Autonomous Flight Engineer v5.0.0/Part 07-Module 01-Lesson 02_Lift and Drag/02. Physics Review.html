02. Physics Review

Physics Review

In flight, a fixed wing aircraft experiences four forces:

  1. Weight (W)
  2. Thrust (T)
  3. Lift (L)
  4. Drag (D)

The plane shown above is in equilibrium. It's flying straight and level at constant speed . The weight and thrust are known:

\begin{aligned} W = & \ 7500 N \\ T = & \ 1000 N \end{aligned}

Based on this information what can you say about the size of the lift force?

SOLUTION: L = \ 7500 N

Now the plane shown above increases the propeller thrust to T = 2500 N . What is the approximate acceleration of the vehicle in the x direction? (assume g = 10 m/s^2 )

SOLUTION: a\_x \approx 2 m/s^2
