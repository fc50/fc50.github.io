04. Coupling

C3 L3 02 Coupling V2

Consider a 2D drone (mass m ) inlined at some angle \phi . How much thrust is required to support this drone in the vertical direction?

SOLUTION: u_1 = \frac{mg}{\cos \phi}

In reality, a drone has a maximum total thrust u_{1,\text{max}} that the 4 rotors can provide.

In terms of u_{1,\text{max}} what is the maximum roll angle, \phi_{\text{max}} , at which a drone can still support itself vertically (without losing altitude)?

SOLUTION: \phi_{\text{max}} = \cos^{-1} \left( \frac{mg}{u_{1,\text{max}}}\right)
